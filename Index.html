About Rajrappa Temple

Rajrappa Temple, also known as the Maa Chhinnamasta Temple, is one of the most famous Hindu pilgrimage destinations in Jharkhand. The temple is dedicated to Goddess Chhinnamasta, a powerful form of Goddess Durga. The temple is situated at the confluence of the Bhairavi (Bhera) River and the Damodar River near Rajrappa in Ramgarh district. Every year thousands of devotees visit the temple to seek blessings.

How to Reach

Nearest Railway Station: Ramgarh Cantonment (Approx. 30 km)

Nearest Airport: Birsa Munda Airport, Ranchi (Approx. 70 km)

The temple is well connected by road from Ranchi, Bokaro, Dhanbad, and Hazaribagh.
